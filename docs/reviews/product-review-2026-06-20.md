## Summary

Reviewed the full product end-to-end in a real browser (Chrome MCP) against the **live backend** with `make seed-demo` data, across all four requested angles. **Every nav area was reached and exercised.** The build is genuinely impressive for a solo MVP: clean, consistent design system; real state machines; correct GST/place-of-supply; **sales→GL accounting that reconciles to the rupee**; strong tenant isolation (RLS), PII-at-rest encryption, and hash-chained tamper-evidence; and a deep, well-thought-out manufacturing module (kanban pipeline, MO operation tracking, 5-bucket QC verdicts, BOM versioning, DAG routing editor).

**No P0/P1 crashes or data-loss bugs found.** The issues are concentrated in two themes worth prioritizing before a customer trial:

1. **Accounting completeness (P2 cluster):** purchases don't post to the GL (no Sundry Creditors; Inventory shows a *negative* balance), stock valuation is ₹0 across the board, and the **Party Khata/ledger is non-functional** (no party-balance/transactions endpoint). For a textile business that lives on khata and supplier balances, these are the most trial-relevant gaps.
2. **Reports period model (P2):** FY label off by one ("Apr 2026 · FY 2025-26"), and three report tabs handle the period three different ways (Daybook empty, P&L all-time, GSTR-1 own picker).

Plus several **FK-not-resolved display bugs** (raw UUIDs shown for cost-centre, MO-operation in job work), the invoice-line **AMOUNT showing the tax-inclusive figure**, the **routing "clone graph"** that doesn't clone, an **overdue-badge miscalc**, and a real **dev-tooling footgun** (`make seed-demo` on the host corrupts the api container's venv).

**Severity tally:** 0 × P0 · **2 × P1** · 14 × P2 · 9 × P3 · 6 positives verified. Full detail below; screenshots in `screens-2026-06-20/`.

> **Deep-dive update (persona pass + live browser validation):** a 6-persona deep-dive (multi-firm owner, manufacturer, trader, accountant, warehouse/sales, karigar/supplier) plus a one-by-one browser re-validation refined several findings and added new ones — see `personas/00-synthesis.md` (cross-cutting roadmap), `personas/01`–`06` (per-persona), and `personas/07-browser-validation.md`. Key refinements now reflected in the table below: **#22** the khata is *built & correct*, just unwired to the party screen (small fix, not a build); **#21** and **#20** are near-one-line root-caused fixes; **#17** escalated to **P1** (the raw UUID + blank GSTIN leak into a fileable ITC-04 return). New: **#24** (wrong voucher number in party statement) and **#25** (a *completed* MO is costed at ₹0).

> **⚠️ Flow-pass update (every-flow state-machine audit, 11 parallel agents):** a full state-machine sweep of every entity lifecycle + cross-entity flow produced a **consolidated bug register at `flows/12-bug-register.md`** (scaffold: `flows/00-flow-machine.md`; 11 per-flow reports `flows/01`–`11`). It surfaced **11 new P1s not visible from the UI**, including three that change the risk profile: **(SEC-1)** cross-org **firm-spoof on writes** — masters/items/accounting/manufacturing lack a firm-in-org guard; a foreign `firm_id` persisted a cross-tenant row live (201); **(SEC-2)** the **audit hash-chain is entirely unpopulated** (0/1612 audit_log, 0/184 stock_ledger) — the claimed tamper-evidence does not exist; **(SEC-3)** **no login/MFA rate-limit or lockout**. Plus manufacturing **phantom-FG** (produced_qty never reconciled to QC), **karigar-in-MO is API-unreachable**, **CANCELLED PO un-cancels via GRN**, **direct invoices never decrement stock**, **no period lock / no voucher reversal / cheque machine unimplemented**, **tax_status ignored in place-of-supply**, and **Vyapar ±₹1 reconcile not implemented**. See the register for all ~30 with severity + `file:line`.

### Top fixes to prioritize for the trial
1. **#22** Party Khata — *quick win, not a build:* embed the already-working `Reports › Party statement` view on the party screen (the data is correct; it just isn't surfaced there). Highest ROI fix in the review.
2. **#18 / #21 / #25** Purchase→GL posting (TASK-041) + stock valuation (one-column fix) + manufacturing conversion cost — together these make the books, stock value, and product cost trustworthy.
3. **#17** (now **P1**) Resolve MO-operation UUID → name *and* fill karigar GSTIN — it's leaking into the ITC-04 statutory return.
4. **#20** Reports period/FY — drop the two hardcoded `'FY 2025-26'` FE constants; unify the per-tab period control.
5. **#4 / #24** Invoice-line AMOUNT (tax-inclusive) and party-statement wrong voucher number — both mislead on money documents.
6. **#13** Routing "clone graph from active version" (silently does nothing).
7. **#1** Dev: shield `/app/.venv` in compose + run `seed-demo` via `docker compose exec`.
